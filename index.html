About

Who are you?

Hey, my name is Kieron, I'm a 22-year-old developer and blogger located in the northwest of England. Since I was young I have always been a lover of computers and tech. I have a great passion for learning all parts of development and you can often find what I have recently been pottering about with on my blog and projects. For my web development, I mostly work on with HTML5, CSS3, ASP.NET MVC6 and Javascript applications. But from time to time I like to challenge myself to learn new technologies such as Ember.js and Python's Django/Flask frameworks.

What else do you like?

I am also a hobbyist in games development. Throughout my degree games development was one of my core passions and since has continued to be. I have lately been into prototyping and creating virtual reality applications in Unity3D. When I am not practicing my programming I like to blog about my experiences, my articles can range from topic to topic but most will centre around what I am currently learning.
